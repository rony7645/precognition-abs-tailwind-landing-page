Suhas Manangi
Co-Founder & CEO

Suhas Manangi spent his career leading safety, integrity, and AI/ML initiatives across Snap, Airbnb, Amazon, Lyft, and Microsoft - most recently as Snap’s Head of Product for Trust & Safety and previously overseeing Airbnb’s AI/ML platform for Trust. Xiuduan Fang
